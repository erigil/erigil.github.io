Hi, I'm Eric!

I'm a Canadian computer enthusiast with a passion for operating systems. A lot of my free time is spent playing around with a select few operating systems and trying to learn from them. I hope to use this knowledge to make my own operating system one day.

I currently have a couple projects on the go. The biggest one is probably the unamed package manager I'm working on. You can find more info on it and any other projects of mine in the "Projects" section of this website. of this website.

A Little History

I've been interested in computers for most of my life. I started out with playing computer games at the age of 5. Strategy and Racing games were my absolute favourites. Five years later I was trying to make my own games on some free development platforms.

When I was around 13 years old I started using Linux, and it completely opened up my eyes to a whole new side of computers. I was obsessed with trying out every Linux distribution that had something unique to offer. That eventually led me the BSDs a year later and other smaller operating systems.

These days I don't constantly switch operating systems and I've settled with FreeBSD one my main computer, NetBSD on a Raspberry Pi, and Alpine Linux on an old toaster of a computer. I also do run a few other distributions and operating systems in virtual machines to play with.
